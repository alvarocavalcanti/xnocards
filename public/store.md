# X, N & O Cards

![X, N & O Cards](https://xnocards.vercel.app/img/hero.png)

X, N, and O Cards are anonymous check-in safety tools for tabletop RPG sessions played online.
Any participant can play a card at any time — no attribution is stored or transmitted.

## The Cards

- **X Card** — Stop or change the current content immediately. No explanation needed.
- **N Card** — Slow down or fade to black. Content is heading somewhere uncomfortable.
- **O Card** — Group check-in. Pause briefly to confirm everyone is comfortable to continue.

## How to use

1. Add the extension from the OBR extension store.
2. Click the **X, N & O Cards** button in the top bar to open the panel.
3. Click a card to broadcast it anonymously to all participants.
4. A full-screen overlay appears for everyone, auto-dismissing after 8 seconds.
   Each participant can also dismiss it manually.

## Privacy

The OBR broadcast API does not include sender identity — no player name or ID is ever
attached to a played card. The tool is fully anonymous by design.
